6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to ensure functionality and improve your experience:

  • Essential cookies: required for the website to function, such as shopping cart and login session cookies. These cannot be disabled through the website because the site cannot function properly without them.
  • Analytics cookies: help us understand how visitors interact with our website. These are activated only with your consent where required.
  • Marketing cookies: used to deliver relevant advertisements. These are activated only with your consent where required.

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through our cookie consent banner or your browser settings.

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject in the European Union, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access (Art. 15) - request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16) - correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17) - request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to restrict processing (Art. 18) - limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20) - receive your data in a structured, commonly used format
  • Right to object (Art. 21) - object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Right to withdraw consent (Art. 7(3)) - withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@infinytree.com.

We will respond within one month as required by GDPR, unless an extension is permitted by law.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures to protect your personal data, including:
